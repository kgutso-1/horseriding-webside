ABOUT US

WELCOME TO WHITERIVER HORSE RIDING ACADEMY

We offer horse riding for all ages and levels of experience, as well as riding lessons including Rodeos, cross country-jumping and horse-racing for a fantastic experience. Towards the end of every year we organize horse racing events for our students to win a prize. We are situated in Whiteriver, in the province of Mpumalanga.
