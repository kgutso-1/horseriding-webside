ACTIVITIES WE PROVIDE IN OUR HORSE RIDING ACADEMY

  • BEGINNERS BEGINNERS AGED 3 - 5 YEARS
    • We cater for children aged from 3 to 5 years in special beginners classes. These lessons are designed to teach the children the basics of riding, whilst having fun. The children are taught in a controlled environment with an experienced instructor. The Lesson consists of 15 minutes of teaching and 15 minutes of supervised ‘free’ time. More information available on request
  • BEGINNERS AGED 6 TO 65
    • Private or group lessons available, on weekdays and weekends. Private lessons can be for ½ hour, ¾ hour or 1 hour in duration. Group lesson are 45 minutes, with between 2 and 5 riders. We teach English Style riding in all disciplines. More information on request.
